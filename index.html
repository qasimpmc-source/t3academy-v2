The methodology

The T3 Method

Three phases that mirror how expertise is actually built. Not passive reading — active, adaptive, and honest about where the gaps are.

01
Phase one
Test
Every session opens with questions — not revision notes. This activates retrieval practice, the most evidence-backed study technique in cognitive science. Your child discovers exactly where their gaps are before a single concept is explained.
02
Phase two
Teach
Targeted teaching for the specific gaps the test revealed. Not a generic lesson — a response to exactly what this student, right now, does not understand. The AI tutor adapts the explanation, chooses the analogy, and checks comprehension before moving on.
03
Phase three
Test again
The same concepts, tested again — harder. This is where understanding becomes memory. Getting it right once is not the same as knowing it. The second test confirms genuine learning and flags anything that still needs work.
