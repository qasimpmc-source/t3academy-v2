The Methodology

The T3 Method

A three-phase cycle that mirrors how memory and expertise are actually built — not passive reading, but active, adaptive engagement.

T1
Phase One
Test
Every session begins with questions — not revision. This activates retrieval practice, the most evidence-backed study technique in cognitive science. You discover exactly where your gaps are before you learn anything.
T2
Phase Two
Teach
After each answer — right or wrong — you receive a detailed explanation, a targeted tip, and a worked example from our AI tutor. The explanation is calibrated to the mistake you just made, not a generic textbook summary.
T3
Phase Three
Test Again
Questions shuffle. Answers re-order. Attempt numbers track your improvement. The same concept returns in a different form until your accuracy climbs through Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum. Mastery is confirmed, not assumed.
